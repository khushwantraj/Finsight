# Finsight

An AI-powered finance platform that unifies bank, broker, and crypto data into one comprehensive dashboard.

## Overview

Finsight empowers retail investors to track, analyze, and optimize portfolios with institutional-grade precision. By integrating data from multiple financial sources, it provides a complete picture of your financial health and investment performance.

## Key Features

### Unified Dashboard
- **Multi-source Integration**: Seamlessly connects bank accounts, brokerage accounts, and crypto wallets
- **Centralized View**: All your financial data in one place
- **Real-time Updates**: Live tracking of all your investments and accounts

### Advanced Analytics & Metrics
Finsight delivers accurate and comprehensive financial metrics:
- **P&L (Profit & Loss)**: Real-time tracking of gains and losses across all accounts
- **CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)**: Measure your investment growth over time
- **XIRR (Extended Internal Rate of Return)**: Accurate returns accounting for irregular cash flows
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted return analysis
- **Risk Metrics**: Comprehensive risk assessment and volatility analysis

### Institutional-Grade Accounting
- **Tax-Lot Accounting**: Precise tracking of cost basis and tax implications
- **Multi-currency Support**: Handle investments across different currencies
- **Transaction History**: Complete audit trail of all financial activities

### Portfolio Management Tools
- **Monte Carlo Simulations**: Run probabilistic scenarios to forecast portfolio performance
- **Rebalancing Simulations**: Optimize asset allocation with intelligent rebalancing strategies
- **Portfolio Optimization**: AI-driven recommendations for better diversification

### Security & Integration
- **Secure Integrations**: Bank-level encryption for all data connections
- **Privacy First**: Your financial data is protected with industry-standard security
- **Reliable Syncing**: Automated data updates from all connected accounts

### Reporting & Insights
- **Clear Reports**: Easy-to-understand visualizations and summaries
- **AI-Driven Insights**: Machine learning-powered recommendations and analysis
- **Custom Dashboards**: Personalized views tailored to your investment strategy

## Benefits

- **Institutional-Grade Precision**: Access to sophisticated financial tools typically reserved for professional investors
- **Time Savings**: Automated data aggregation eliminates manual tracking
- **Better Decisions**: Data-driven insights help you make informed investment choices
- **Complete Picture**: Understand your entire financial position across all asset classes
- **Tax Optimization**: Tax-lot accounting helps minimize tax liabilities
